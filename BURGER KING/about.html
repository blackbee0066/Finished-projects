Our History

Every day, more than 11 million guests visit Burger King restaurants around the world. And they do so because our restaurants are known for serving high-quality, great-tasting burgers. Founded in 1954, Burger King is the second largest fast food hamburger chain in the world.

Home of the Whopper

The famous burger is the WHOPPER. The beef is flame-grilled which gives it its particular flavor. The original Home of the Whopper, our commitment to premium ingredients, signature recipes, and family-friendly dining experiences is what has defined our brand for more than 50 successful years.
